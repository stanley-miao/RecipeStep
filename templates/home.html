About Recipe Step

RecipeStep is a web application that allows users to easily run through the steps of their recipes in a presentable format. RecipeStep works to cut down on screen clutter when following a recipe by only presenting one step at a time. To get started, you can create an account by selecting “log in” and “create an account.” By entering in the required information and clicking submit, you can access your dashboard. To add a recipe, click the “add recipe” button and enter in a recipe name. Then, you can directly copy and paste your recipe from any website into the recipe box. Press “enter,” and you’ve added your recipe! Simple, right?
